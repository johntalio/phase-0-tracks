Animal Order, Family Interesting Fact(s)
Penguin Sphenisciformes, Spheniscidae The word penguin first appears in the 16th century as a synonym for great auk.
Peacock Mantis Shrimp Stomatopoda, Odontodactylidae Their ability to see circularly polarized light has led to studies to determine if the mechanisms by which their eyes operate can be replicated for use in reading CDs and other such devices.
Elephant Proboscidea, Elephantidae Elephants use seismic commucanication over long distances, appear to have self-awarenesss, and show empathy for dying or dead individuals of their kind.
